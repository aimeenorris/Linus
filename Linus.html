All About Linus

Linus was born on November 26th, 2016. He is now days old.

We adopted Linus on January 25th, 2017, which means we have had him in our home for days.

We adopted Linus from Cause For Paws which is a local rescue group for dogs in the Raleigh area. He was found on the street along with a brother and sister. Cause for Paws named the trio after characters from the Peanuts. We decided to keep Linus' name because we thought it fit him. Linus is a great dog!
